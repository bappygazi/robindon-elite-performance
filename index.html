What is Gain The Edge?

Gain the Edge Hockey Training is a fully online coaching program for Hockey players like you who want to take their game on the ice to the next level and give themselves the edge over their competition.

Inside the program you'll get

Weekly workouts (Loaded inside an easy to use app) take the guesswork out of what to do in the gym. Whether you are working out at a gym facility, or in your basement we will help you Gain the Edge!

The workouts inside the program are designed around building your bodies foundation. Hockey players need to be strong in all planes of motion. A weakness anywhere can lead to injury and/or a lag in your game. Inside this program we'll train your body in every plane to make sure that regardless of what position you play or what position your body gets placed into during a game you'll be ready!

A Nutrition Guide (Downloadable PDF) to help you learn how to fuel your body the way it needs, to help you recover after your workouts and games. To teach you a simple method to get you to meet your individual goals. Whether your goal is to gain more muscle while you get stronger and faster, or if it's to lose a little bit of fat while getting stronger and faster! Either way, you'll learn how to do it!

Learning the basics of Nutrition will not only set you up on the Ice and the weight room but it will set you up for a healthier life !

Nutrition is incredibly important and inside the program we'll learn more about why it's so important!

A weekly coaching call with me. Here we'll go over your week, how things went for you inside the gym, your nutrition, your goals moving forward and how we can improve things in your life to help you increase your performance not only on the ice but in all areas of your life.

  • - Goal Setting
  • - Self Reflection
  • - Time Management
  • - Communication Skills- Goal Setting
  • - Self Reflection
  • - Time Management
  • - Communication Skills

These calls are a great resource for you to keep pushing to be better!

Video Breakdown - Each week you’ll send a video of 1-3 shifts from your players game from the weekend or from past games that you want us to break down. We will dissect the video and give you feedback on things to fix, things that they did well and how to improve their game on both sides of the puck.

One of the greatest advantages of living in this day and age is the advancement in technology. Being able to relive each and every second of our games. Rewind and slow down everything to improve all tiny details of our game. Hockey IQ is such a crucial part of hockey. It doesn’t matter how good your skills are if you don’t know where to be on the ice during the play, or where to be in different situations you’ll look lost and basically be putting your team a man down every time you are out there.
